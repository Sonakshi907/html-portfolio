The best movies according to Sonakshi

My top three(3) movies of all time!

Raid

Raid is a Hindi-language thriller drama that tells the story of an incorruptible Income Tax officer who, after receiving an anonymous tip, plans a raid on a powerful political leader's home and businesses

The Last Of Us

The Last of Us tells the story of Joel and Ellie, two survivors in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a fungal infection that turns humans into monstrous creatures

Raid 2

Raid 2 is a crime thriller movie. The story follows IRS officer Amay Patnaik as he investigates another case of crime, this time involving a powerful politician Dada Manohar Bhai , in a fictional state called Bhoj.
